## 2. Logo Clear-Space Guidelines (Tiered System)

1. **Standard Applications – “K” spacing**  
   - Reference: Height of the “K” in K7  
   - Use: Print, website headers, presentations, signage  

2. **Compact Applications – “Two fullstops” spacing**  
   - Reference: Width of two fullstops (“..”) in K7  
   - Use: Sub-brand logos, email headers, small digital assets  

3. **Extra-Compact Applications – “One fullstop” spacing**  
   - Reference: Width of one fullstop (“.”) in K7  
   - Use: Favicons, app icons, tight layouts  

**Notes:**  
- Maintain spacing hierarchy consistently  
- Keep clear-space zones free from other visual elements
