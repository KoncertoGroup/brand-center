## 6. Imagery & Iconography

- Photography: Human, natural, collaborative, clear and positive  
- Illustrations: Minimal geometric forms; reinforce brand orchestration theme  
- Icons: Outline or two-tone using neutrals + accent highlights  
- Motifs: Subtle geometric patterns representing flow, connectivity, and progress
